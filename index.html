Biography

It all started with The Prodigy, they were his first music influence, later on Antoine Clamaran and Carl Cox, today Dubfire, Carola, Luciano. He found the world of DJing fascinating with all the different genres, from techno then house and dark progressive and finally minimal-tech-house which he now plays and produces. His tracks were played by Dubfire, Hawtin, Carola, Umek, Varela, Spartaque, Oxia... but the biggest support comes from Deeperfect owner Stefano Noferini.
Has signings with some major minimal labels in the business: Little Helpers, Innocent, Distance, New Violence, Krad, Sibilla, Nastyfunk, Little Festival, 12+1 London, Tres14, Datagroove, Techaway, Conceptual, Kootz, Psicodelica, Mushroom Smile, Nopreset, Go Deeva...
